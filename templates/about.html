About Us

We are a web-based platform that aims to help SMEs reduce their carbon footprint. We provide information about various green certifications and connect businesses with eco-friendly service providers.

Our Mission

Our mission is to simplify the journey to net-zero emissions for SMEs by providing them with the resources and support they need to make sustainable changes to their business practices.

Our Vision

We envision a future where all businesses, regardless of their size, are able to operate in a way that is environmentally sustainable and contributes to the fight against climate change.

Our Team

We are a team of passionate individuals who believe in the power of small businesses to make a big impact on the environment. We come from diverse backgrounds and bring a wide range of expertise to our work.
